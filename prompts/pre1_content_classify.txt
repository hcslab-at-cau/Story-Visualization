You are classifying EPUB-extracted paragraphs into content types.

# GOAL
- For EACH sentence, assign a content_type and whether it is story text.

# content_type OPTIONS
- front_matter: title/author/edition/publisher/copyright
- toc: contents/table of contents entries
- chapter_heading: chapter number/title lines (e.g., 'CHAPTER I. ...')
- section_heading: other headings/subheadings
- epigraph: short quote/poem placed before a chapter/section
- narrative: actual story prose
- non_narrative_other: footnotes/page numbers/formatting artifacts

# RULES
- narrative is the ONLY type with is_story_text=true.
- Headings are NOT story text, but keep them for anchoring.
- toc lines often look like lists of chapter titles.

# OUTPUT JSON SCHEMA
{{
  "units": [
    {{
      "sid": <int>,
      "content_type": "front_matter|toc|chapter_heading|section_heading|epigraph|narrative|non_narrative_other",
      "is_story_text": <true|false>
    }}
  ]
}}

# INPUT
SENTENCES:
{buffer_sentences}
